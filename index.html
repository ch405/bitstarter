SwuSwu is a social network site designed for simple and fast online swap and trade. You want to make room on your shelves and get rid of unnecessary items in exchange for something useful? We want to create a web application to do this with just a few mouse clicks. All you need to do is login, take a picture of your item(s) with your cell phone or web camera, write a shor description an voila!, your item(s) are instantly added to your account where other users can check them out and give you an offer.

Focus of SwuSwu is on swaping stuff or services, but that doesn't exclude trading for money. If you have many offers, than you can create auction to get the most for your item. If you don't have any offer, than you can use integrated marketing system to increase your chances for swap or sale. All users will be ranked by their customers for their credibility and quality of service and with higher rank you get special site features (like creating your own virtual store). This project is in the first phase of development, so we present only a fraction of the possibilites offered by SwuSwu so please support us.
